User Privacy Policy

At Kozy Baby, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Data Collection and Storage

We do not store any personal data on the web. All information and data generated by the app are stored locally on your phone. Your data remains private and secure within your device, and we do not transmit or store it on any servers or third-party services.

Data Usage

The app only uses data stored locally on your phone to provide the features and functionality you request. We do not access or share any of your data with external entities.

Data Security

Since your data is stored only on your phone, it is protected by the security measures of your device. We encourage you to ensure that your phone is secured with a password, fingerprint, or other security features provided by your operating system.

No Web Data Storage

To clarify, no data collected by the app is stored on any web servers. Everything remains strictly on your device, providing you with full control over your personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please feel free to contact us at luckyltk9@gmail.com.
